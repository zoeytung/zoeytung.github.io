GO Global Database

The most comprehensive global database of NGOs today.

The GO Global Database began as a compilation of registered charity databases from different national government agencies across the world. By compiling an active directory of NGOs around the world and their projects we believe better collaborations between NGOs can be achieved. The database is the most comprehensive collection of international NGOs today. You will find updated details about each organizationâ€™s project and capabilities in each NGO's profile. These active collections of on-going projects allow non-profit organizations around the world to update and connect with other organizations that have capabilities that can complement or enrich current programs.

By pooling together the limited and different capabilities of individual NGOs, together they can execute and deliver better programs and services. By joining the GO Global Database, your organization will join a community of non-profit organizations that want to enhace their operations. The database is only the first step

Importance of the GO Local App

The GO Local App will enable non-profit practionners to ask questions and share experiences contributing to the constructive NGO community and further building trust and communications. By building transparent communication and trust, a sustainable and successful partnership between non-profit organizations will be more likely.

GO Local brings you a community platform to share and discuss right on to your cellphone. Organizations around the world can belong to the GO NGO community by engaging on this app.

So in conclusion ...

Enlarge the capacity of your NGO by updating your profile on the GO NGO Database and actively engage with other organizations using the GO Local app. Together we can build a constructive community of NGOs!!
