Why Connect?

The Goal

Collaboration is key between non-profit organizations because by pooling together knowledge, capacities and expertise, organizations will be even more empowered to further achieve their missions. NGOs more than any other types of organizations have been operating with limited resources to deliver their mandates. By engaging in constructive collaborations they can expand their capacities and achieve more scope in their projects.

Collabortions do not take place over night, GO NGO's mandate is to faciliate the likelyhood of successful and lasting partnerships. Put it more simply,if NGOs operate in isolation from one another their IMPACT becomes isolated and is reduced.
We know that collaboration between organizations does not ensure a successful delivery of their mandate nor is a solution to all the existing inefficiencies in the industry. The key for collaboration between organizations to serve NGOs better is by a specific type of collaboration, which is strong and trusting collaborations. This type of unique collaboration must be established organically and willingly between two organizations. Both collaborating NGOs much be transparent in how they would like their partners to enrich their mandate and what they can offer to their partners. Therefore, GO NGO is a tool with the intentions to ensure better possibilities for collaborations and more transparency between NGOs. GO NGO wants to unite global NGOs into a constructive and collaborative community. This community will empower all NGOs to deliver and accomplish their missions more efficiently.

About the Founders

Sam and Zoey are both students in International Development Studies at McGill University. They started GO NGO to address the need to improve efficiencies in the day to day operations of the NGO sector. They goal is to create a collaborative community of non-profit organizations.
