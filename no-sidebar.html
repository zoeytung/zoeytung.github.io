Why Connect?

The Goal

Collaboration is key between non-profit organizations because by pooling together knowledge, capacities and expertise, organizations will be even more empowered to further achieve their missions. NGOs more than any other types of organizations have been operating with limited resources to deliver their mandates. By engaging in constructive collaborations they can expand their capacities and achieve more scope in their projects.

Collabortions do not take place over night, GO NGO's mandate is to faciliate the likelyhood of successful and lasting partnerships.

About the Founders

Sam and Zoey are both students in International Development Studies at McGill University. They started NGO Go Global in their final year at university as a project for their Social Entrepreneurship class. They both saw a need to improve efficiencies in day to day NGO work.
